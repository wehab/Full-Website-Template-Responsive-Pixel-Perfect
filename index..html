THE CONNECTIVITY WEB

BUILT is the connectivity web in the Real Estate Development, Design, and Construction Industry. We are an exclusive membership-based
organization with four nationwide chapters – Georgia, the Carolinas, North Texas, and Colorado (opening January 2019). Each chapter hosts
10 to12 events per year which includes service projects, fundraisers, special dinner events, educational seminars, clay shoots, luncheons,
happy hours, and two annual golf tournaments (including The Annual Invitational held each fall). BUILT's members are committed to one
another’s personal and professional success.

MISSION

BUILT's mission is to grow professional connections and drive revenue for our member firms by creating thoughtful and meaningful experiences and events.

VALUES

BUILT's core values are simple -- we are founded on leadership, relationships, knowledge, trust, and service. Our members embody these values, and are fun, energetic, as well as likeable. We pride ourselves on being service-oriented and are committed to giving back to our peers and our communities.
